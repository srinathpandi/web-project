A Day Of FUN Awaits You...!




One of the biggest entertainment parks in the country, MGM Dizzee World in Mahabalipuram offers high-energy entertainment facilities to guests of all age groups. They provide several amusement parks that boast of exhilarating rides that also further adhere to international safety standards and norms. This entire park is designed by Italian architects. It is commodiously spaced on huge lush green lands and is surrounded by scenic blue beaches of Muttukadu. This is one of the largest theme parks that attracts more than one million eager customers on an annual basis. This amusement park has created a household name for itself in the whole of Tamil Nadu as well as pan-India. They have officially earned an ISO/14001: 2004 and OHSAS18001: 2007 certification and have also earned 5553 votes on the listings page. This park can be easily accessed as it is near the toll plaza and is close to Muttukadu Bus Station. Undoubtedly it is one of the best Amusement Parks in Chennai.
